God Emperor Trump refers to fantasy depictions of United States President Donald Trump as a ruler of the entire world often who is often shown wearing the armor of the immortal character Emperor of Mankind[2] from the miniature wargame Warhammer 40,000.
The exact origin of the God Emperor Trump character is unclear. On June 16th, 2015, an image of Trump's face photoshopped on to a Warhammer 40,000 soldier was submitted to 4chan's /pol/ board along with greentext described Trump as "God Emperor" and "Imperium of Man".
